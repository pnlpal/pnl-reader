Thank you for using PNL Reader 2.0!

PNL Reader is an open-source project, and you can follow its development on GitHub.

On this options page, you can customize the settings. All changes will apply globally across all websites.

On the webpage where you use PNL Reader, you can also override these settings for that specific website. Just click the PNL Reader icon in the browser toolbar to activate the Reader Mode and access the settings for that site.

I’m the developer behind this app, creating tools for educational purposes and independently sharing them with the world. If you enjoy using PNL Reader, I’d greatly appreciate your support -- please share it with others! Your help makes a big difference.

PNL Reader was inspired by real user needs and my own search for a better reading experience online. It all began with a community discussion about the challenges of finding a simple, privacy-friendly reader mode that works seamlessly with Dictionariez -- another browser extension I created for instant dictionary lookup. With Dictionariez, you can double-click any word on a webpage to see its meaning and open a full dictionary entry in a popup.



Motivated by feedback from users who wanted a no-nonsense reader mode that respects privacy and integrates well with Dictionariez, I set out to build PNL Reader. It’s designed to make reading easier, distraction-free, and more enjoyable while keeping your privacy in mind.

I value your feedback!

Have suggestions or feature requests? Feel free to share them on GitHub or join the discussion in the Programming N' Language Community . Your input is invaluable in improving this project! You can also connect with me on Discord or follow me on BlueSky .
